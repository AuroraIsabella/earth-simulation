What is Earth Simulation

A speculative future-history platform.

Earth Simulation is not just a landing page. It is a narrative interface for exploring how climate systems, adaptive organisms, synthetic infrastructure, and post-human ecologies might interact over centuries, millennia, and geological time.

Planetary Systems

Track atmosphere, oceans, ice, deserts, forests, and migration corridors as connected simulation layers.

Future-History Lens

Speculative Science

Blend plausible adaptation, ecological imagination, and cinematic science fiction into a fast static experience.

Project vision

From static concept to living simulation atlas.

The long-term vision is an interactive simulation where visitors can generate ecosystems with AI, compare future civilization scenarios, build user-created timelines, and eventually explore a real-time 3D Earth visualization. V3 expands the foundation with interactive era switching while keeping the experience fast, static, and ready for Vercel.
